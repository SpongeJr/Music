This is an example of using Eric Blossom's chord transposer plugin for jQuery. Note that you will have to point to your copy of jQuery for this to work. With it one can transpose chords in a song sheet containing chords and lyrics.

in D


D 
O, I went down south for to see my Sal,
D A 
Sing polly wolly doodle all day,
A 
My Sally she's a spunky gal,
A D 
Sing polly wolly doodle all the day.
